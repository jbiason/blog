## Keybinds

### Clocking

- Clocking in: `C-c` `C-x` `C-i`
- Clocking out: `C-c` `C-x` `C-o`

### State

- Next state: `C-c` `C-t`
- Next state: `C-right`
- Previous state: `C-left`

### Tagging

- `C-c` `C-q`
